Yifan Zhou

Hi, I am a Ph.D. student at Interactive Robotics Lab, Arizona State University, advised by Prof. Heni Ben Amor. My research interests include language-conditioned robot manipulation and robot perception.

Prior to starting Ph.D., I received my M.S. at Carnegie Mellon University and B.E. at Southwest Jiaotong University. During my undergraduate period, I worked with Prof. Xiao Wu on unsupervised image-to-image generation.
